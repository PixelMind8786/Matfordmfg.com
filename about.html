Our Story

Established in 2009 in Walsall, West Midlands, Matford Manufacturing has grown from a small precision engineering workshop into a trusted partner for manufacturers across the UK.

As a family-run business, we understand that relationships matter. Our focus has always been on delivering exceptional customer service alongside precision components that meet the most demanding specifications.

Today, we operate from our purpose-built facility in Premier Business Park, equipped with state-of-the-art Mori NLX 2500 CNC turning centres. This investment in advanced technology, combined with our ISO 9001 certified quality management system, enables us to deliver consistently excellent results on even the tightest deadlines.

We're proud of our West Midlands engineering heritage and remain committed to the values that have driven our success: precision, reliability, and genuine partnership with every customer we serve.

Our Facility

Our modern facility at Premier Business Park in Walsall is equipped with the latest CNC turning technology, enabling us to handle complex components with exceptional precision.

We've invested heavily in Mori NLX 2500 turning centres – machines renowned for their accuracy and reliability. With multi-axis capability, milling, and drilling in a single setup, we can produce complex geometries efficiently and cost-effectively.

Our lights-out automation capability means we can run production around the clock, helping us meet demanding delivery schedules while maintaining the consistency that our customers expect.
